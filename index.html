GTA 6 Map & Location

Confirmed Map Details

Based on the official trailer and Rockstar's statements, GTA 6 will be set in the state of Leonida, featuring:

  • Vice City: A modern recreation of Miami and Miami Beach with detailed urban landscapes, skyscrapers, and iconic locations.
  • Port Gellhorn: A smaller coastal town shown in the trailer, likely based on Port St. Lucie or Key West.
  • Everglades-inspired area: Swamplands and wilderness regions similar to the Florida Everglades.
  • Keys-like islands: A string of islands resembling the Florida Keys, with connecting bridges and unique environments.
  • Beaches and coastal areas: Extensive beachfront properties, piers, and recreational areas.

Map Size Comparison

Based on analysis of the trailer footage and industry reports, GTA 6's map is expected to be approximately 1.5 to 2 times larger than GTA 5's Los Santos map, making it the largest and most detailed open world in Rockstar's history.
